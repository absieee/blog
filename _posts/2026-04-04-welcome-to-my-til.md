I wanted a place to capture useful things before they disappeared into notes apps, half-written docs, or browser tabs I was definitely going to revisit.

This site is intentionally lightweight. If an idea is worth writing down, it should be easy to publish. That means:

- short posts are welcome
- rough edges are fine
- usefulness matters more than polish

Expect notes on product thinking, technical concepts I want to remember, and the occasional reflection on building things.
