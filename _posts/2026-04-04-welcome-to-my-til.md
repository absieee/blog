I wanted a place on the internet to capture useful things before they disappeared into various notes apps, half-written docs, or browser tabs I was definitely never going to revisit.

This site is intentionally lightweight. If an idea is worth writing down, it should be easy to publish. 

That means:
- short posts
- rough edges
- usefulness over polish

Expect notes on product thinking, technical concepts I want to remember, and a lot of reflection on building things and life in general.
