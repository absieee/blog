## Why this exists

I wanted a place to publish small ideas without turning every thought into a polished article. This blog is for:

- product lessons
- technical notes
- things learned while building
- useful links and reminders for future me

## How I'll use it

Most posts should stay short. If something wants to become a proper essay, it can, but the default should be quick and useful.
